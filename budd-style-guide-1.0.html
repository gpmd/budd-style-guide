4. Typography In Use

Welcome

Budd has been making and supplying the finest bespoke, made to measure and ready to wear shirts from its shop in the Piccadilly Arcade for over one hundred years. A purveyor and specialist of the very best menswear accessories and formal wear, Budd celebrates British quality, craftsmanship and tradition, sourcing and making all of its products from within the UK.

The Shop

Budd has been located in the Piccadilly Arcade since the companys’ inception in 1910 and forms part of the elegant thoroughfare of shops that leads into Jermyn Street. Like all of Mayfair’s traditional arcade boutiques, the shop is tiny, but its size belies the wealth of goodies that can be found inside.

Our prime offering is shirts. We make all our shirts by hand at our workshop here in England. We are also one of the few remaining West End shirtmakers to still have its own cutting room on the premises.
